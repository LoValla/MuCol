In this repo, there are 4 different folders:
- mucoll-benchmarks, which contains the setting for digitisation and reconstruction at the Muon Collider with Marlin.
- piguns, to generate, analyse and have a look at pi guns track and reconstruction efficiencies.
- tauguns, to generate, analyse and have a look at tau guns track and reconstruction efficiencies.
- HvsBkg, to compare and analyse the H->tautau signal and the main backgrounds
